Betting Strategy Guide

1. Bankroll Management

The foundation of profitable betting is proper bankroll management:

  • Unit System: Bet 1-5% of your total bankroll per wager
  • High Confidence: 3-5% of bankroll (60%+ hit probability)
  • Medium Confidence: 2-3% of bankroll (50-59% hit probability)
  • Low/Speculative: 1-2% of bankroll (under 50% hit probability)
Example: With a $300 bankroll, your max bet should be $15 (5%) on high-confidence plays

2. Player Diversification

Avoid correlation risk by diversifying your selections:

  • Different Teams: Don't stack multiple players from the same team
  • Different Games: Spread picks across multiple matchups
  • Different Stat Types: Mix hitting, pitching, and fielding props
  • Avoid Same-Game Parlays: One bad game kills multiple legs

3. Optimal Slip Sizing

Structure your bet slips for maximum profitability:

  • 2-4 Pick Maximum: Sweet spot for risk vs. reward
  • Focus on Expected Value: Target positive EV over big payouts
  • Quality Over Quantity: Better to place fewer, well-researched bets
  • Single Bets: Consider straight bets on high-confidence props
